Privacy policy

Loravo provides an application and services that can connect to third-party accounts such as Gmail, Outlook, and Yahoo when a connection is explicitly enabled. Loravo also offers optional LXT AI features.

This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and the choices available.

1. Information collected

Information may include:

  • Account identifiers (email address and/or phone number).
  • Internal account ID (for example, Firebase UID).
  • User-submitted content (messages, prompts, preferences).
  • Optional location context (when enabled for weather or news features).
  • Connected provider tokens (OAuth tokens for Gmail, Outlook, or Yahoo after connection).
  • Diagnostics and security data necessary to operate and protect the service.

2. How information is used

  • To provide requested features (inbox viewing, summaries, organization, alerts).
  • To operate, protect, and secure Loravo.
  • To maintain and improve reliability and performance.

3. Connected providers

Access to Gmail, Outlook, or Yahoo occurs only after explicit authorization. Connections may be removed at any time within the application settings.

4. LXT AI

LXT is Loravo’s AI system used to generate summaries and assistant-style workflows. Processing occurs only as required to complete tasks initiated by the user.

5. Sharing

Personal information is not sold. Data is shared only with service providers necessary to operate Loravo or with connected third-party APIs that you authorize.

6. Data retention

Data is retained only as necessary to provide the service and comply with applicable legal obligations. Deletion requests may be submitted via the contact below.
