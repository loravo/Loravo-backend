Privacy Policy
Security-first • OAuth-based • User-controlled

Privacy, by design.

Loravo helps you manage your life and communications through connected services (Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo) and LXT AI features. Your data remains yours — you control what’s connected, what’s accessed, and what’s processed.

Last updated: 2026-02-17 Contact: loravolxt@yahoo.com Integrations: Gmail • Outlook • Yahoo AI: LXT

1) What Loravo is

Loravo (“we”, “us”, “our”) is an application that can connect to third-party providers you authorize (such as Google/Gmail, Microsoft/Outlook, and Yahoo) to provide features you request — including inbox viewing, email tools, summaries, organization, and assistant-style workflows using LXT.

Core promise: We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your connected email data to build advertising profiles. We only access provider data when you explicitly connect and request features that require it.

2) Information we collect

Account & identifiers Required
  • Email address and/or phone number (depending on sign-in method).
  • Internal account ID (e.g., Firebase UID) to associate your settings and connections.
Connected provider tokens OAuth
  • OAuth access/refresh tokens for Gmail, Outlook, or Yahoo only when you connect.
  • Token expiry timestamps and provider connection status.
  • We never store your provider password.
Email data (when you use email features) User requested
  • Inbox metadata: sender, subject, date, message ID, and other standard headers.
  • Message content: body text/HTML, only when you open a message or request a summary/draft.
  • Sending/replying: recipient address, subject line, and content you provide.

We aim to minimize data collection and use only what’s necessary to deliver the specific feature you requested.

3) How we use your information

  • To connect and maintain integrations you authorize (Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo).
  • To display your inbox and messages inside Loravo.
  • To provide email tools like search, read, summarize, draft replies, send, and organize.
  • To provide assistant features using LXT based on the inputs you submit (messages, prompts, selected emails).
  • To operate, secure, debug, and improve reliability/performance (e.g., error logs, rate limits).
LXT AI processing On-demand

LXT processes information you provide to generate outputs (summaries, drafts, decisions, structured responses). We limit processing to the content required to fulfill the request.

We do not use your connected email content to train a generalized public AI model.

What we do NOT do Strict
  • No selling personal information.
  • No ad targeting segments from mailbox data.
  • No “engagement tracking” for marketing profiling.
  • No sharing mailbox content with third parties for unrelated purposes.

4) Provider-specific notes

Gmail (Google)

If you connect Gmail, Loravo uses Google’s OAuth flow. Access is limited to scopes you authorize. You can revoke access at any time in your Google Account security settings.

Outlook (Microsoft)

If you connect Outlook, Loravo uses Microsoft OAuth (typically Microsoft Graph) and only accesses the mailbox data needed for features you request. You can revoke access in your Microsoft account settings.

Yahoo

If you connect Yahoo, Loravo uses Yahoo OAuth2. Access to IMAP/CardDav/CalDav may require Yahoo approval for restricted scopes. You can revoke access in your Yahoo account settings.

User control

You can disconnect any provider from within Loravo. When disconnected, we stop using the provider tokens.

5) Sharing & third parties

Loravo does not sell personal information. We only share limited data with:

  • Infrastructure providers (hosting, databases) strictly to operate Loravo.
  • Connected providers (Google/Microsoft/Yahoo) when you use features requiring their APIs.

We do not share mailbox data with third-party advertisers or data brokers.

6) Security

  • Encryption in transit using HTTPS/TLS.
  • Tokens stored securely and used only for authorized provider requests.
  • Access controls to reduce internal access to sensitive information.
  • We aim to minimize data stored and retain only what’s necessary to provide the service.

7) Data retention

We retain data as needed to operate the service and provide features you request. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

8) Legal compliance & your rights

We comply with applicable privacy laws (including GDPR and CCPA where applicable). Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, and to withdraw consent.

9) Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top reflects the latest version.
