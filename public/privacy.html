Privacy policy

(Previous version)

Loravo provides an application and services that can connect to third-party accounts (such as Gmail, Outlook, and Yahoo) when a connection is explicitly enabled. Loravo also offers optional LXT AI features.

This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and the choices available.

1. Information collected

Information may be collected in the following categories:

  • Account identifiers (email address and/or phone number depending on sign-in method).
  • Internal account ID (for example, a Firebase UID) to associate settings and connected providers.
  • User-submitted content (messages, prompts, preferences, and actions performed inside the app).
  • Optional location context (only when location is enabled for local weather/news features).
  • Connected provider tokens (OAuth access/refresh tokens) for Gmail, Outlook, or Yahoo only after a provider is connected.
  • Diagnostics & security data (limited logs needed to operate, protect, and improve reliability).

2. How information is used

  • To provide features requested (inbox viewing, email tools, summaries, organization, alerts).
  • To operate and secure Loravo (fraud prevention, abuse detection, reliability).
  • To maintain and improve performance (debugging, crash fixes, service monitoring).

3. Connected email providers

When a provider (Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo) is connected, Loravo accesses provider data only to deliver the features requested. Permissions depend on what is granted during the provider’s OAuth consent flow.

  • Provider access can be disconnected at any time in Settings.
  • Tokens are stored to keep a connection working until it is disconnected or revoked.
  • Loravo does not use connected email data for advertising profiling or targeting.

4. LXT AI features

LXT is Loravo’s AI system used to generate summaries, organization suggestions, and assistant workflows. LXT processes only the content necessary to complete requested tasks.

5. Sharing

Loravo does not sell personal information. Data may be shared only with:

  • Service providers used to operate Loravo (hosting, databases, monitoring) under confidentiality and security controls.
  • Third-party APIs that are explicitly connected (for example, Google or Microsoft, and Yahoo where supported).

6. Data retention

Data is retained only as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, and maintain security. Provider connections can be removed in Settings. Data deletion requests can be submitted using the contact below.

7. Security

Reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards are used to protect information. No system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

8. Your choices

  • Disconnect providers at any time from Settings.
  • Control optional permissions such as location through device settings.
  • Request deletion of account data using the contact below.

9. Contact

Email: loravolxt@yahoo.com
