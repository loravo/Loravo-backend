LORAVO
Privacy Policy

Privacy, by design.

Loravo helps you manage communications through connected services (Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo) and LXT AI features. Your data remains user-controlled — connections can be removed at any time.

Core promise: Loravo does not sell personal information. Connected email data is not used to build advertising profiles. Provider data is accessed only when you explicitly connect a provider and request a feature that requires it.
1) What Loravo is

Loravo is an application that can connect to third-party providers you authorize (such as Gmail, Outlook, and Yahoo) to deliver features you request — including inbox viewing, email tools, summaries, organization, and assistant-style workflows using LXT.

2) Information we collect

Account & identifiers

  • Email address and/or phone number (depending on sign-in method).
  • Internal account ID (e.g., Firebase UID) to associate settings and connections.

Connected provider tokens

  • OAuth access/refresh tokens for Gmail, Outlook, or Yahoo only when you connect a provider.
  • Token expiry timestamps and connection status for reliability.

Content you request Loravo to process

  • Email metadata/content only when loading inbox, reading a message, or generating a summary you request.
  • Messages and preferences you choose to submit to the app.

Optional context

  • Location only when enabled for local weather/news features.
  • Device & diagnostics signals for security and performance (limited, non-advertising use).
3) How we use information
  • To provide app features you request (email tools, summaries, alerts, organization).
  • To operate, secure, and maintain the service.
  • To improve reliability and performance.
4) Sharing

Loravo does not sell your personal information. We only share data with service providers necessary to operate Loravo (hosting, databases) and with third-party APIs you explicitly connect (e.g., Google/Microsoft/Yahoo). We do not share connected mail data for advertising, profiling, or audience targeting.

5) Data retention

We retain data as needed to provide the service. You may disconnect providers at any time, which revokes access going forward. You may request deletion by contacting us.

6) Contact

Email: loravolxt@yahoo.com

Loravo supports sign-in via Apple and Google. Provider connections (Gmail/Outlook/Yahoo) use OAuth where available and are always user-initiated.
